Welcome to PawPrint. A development tracker for teams to review and approve git commits before merging back with the main code.

The general workflow looks something like this...

  • Create your own branch, do some work, and get a final (head) commit hash.
  • Once complete, make a ticket in PawPrint.
  • Manager will review commit, and either:
    • Comment for corrections.
    • Approve commits.
  • Once approved, it is the developers job to apply changes to relevant branches, and mark ticket as confirm.
  • Reviewer will verify changes have been applied correctly and complete the ticket.
  • Only in rare situations will a ticket need to be closed.

It must be noted this is purely a tracker, and does not prevent misuse of git. It is your own responsibility to not blow stuff up into a giant ball of flames.
